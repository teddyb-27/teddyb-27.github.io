Fettuccine Alfredo

  • Ingredients

  • 24 ounces dry fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 pint heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 dash garlic salt
  • 3/4 cup grated Romano cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

  1. Directions

  2. Boil a large pot of salted water. Add fettuccine pasta and cook for 8-10 minutes and then drain the water.
  3. Melt butter and cream in a large saucepan over low heat; add salt, pepper, and garlic salt.
  4. Increase the heat to medium; stir in grated Romano and Parmesan cheese until melted and sauce has thickened.
  5. Add cooked pasta to sauce and toss until thoroughly coated; serve immediately.
